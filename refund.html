Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

OTMGPT offers a 30-day money-back guarantee for new paid subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within your first 30 days of paid access, we'll refund your payment in full — no questions asked.

Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • You must submit your refund request within 30 days of your first paid charge
  • Your account must not be in violation of our Terms of Service
  • The refund applies to your most recent subscription payment only

How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@otmgpt.com with:

  • The email address on your account
  • Your reason for requesting a refund (optional but appreciated)

Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days back to your original payment method.

Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically trigger a refund. Refunds are only issued under the 30-day guarantee terms above.

Digital Products

Downloadable digital products (including the Starter Blueprint) are non-refundable once downloaded.
