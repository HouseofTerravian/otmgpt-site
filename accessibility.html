ADA Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Our Commitment

OutTheMudGPT (OTMGPT.com) is committed to providing a website and platform that is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios for text readability
  • Keyboard navigation support throughout the platform
  • Descriptive link text and alt attributes for images
  • Responsive design for use on all devices

Known Limitations

We are actively working to identify and resolve accessibility gaps. Some third-party integrations may have their own accessibility limitations outside our direct control.

Feedback & Assistance

If you experience difficulty accessing any portion of our website or platform, please contact us. We will work to provide the content or service in an alternative format.

Accessibility contact: accessibility@otmgpt.com

General support: support@otmgpt.com

We will respond to accessibility requests within 5 business days.
